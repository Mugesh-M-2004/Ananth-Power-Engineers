Hydraulic Press Brake

The stable, robust welded steel frame, crafted from ultrasonically and tensile-tested IS 2062 plate, meets the stringent IMTMA standards, ensuring unmatched durability and precision. With fast approach and return speeds that minimize cycle time, coupled with a slow pressing speed for superior stroke control and bending accuracy, productivity is significantly enhanced.

The system boasts a repeatability of ± 0.05mm for ram positioning. Its compact cylinders, low-pressure system, and synchronized cylinders via a welded torque tube provide exceptional rigidity and load-bearing capacity. Enhanced with NC upgradability and premium hydraulic components, it ensures reliable performance and easy operation.

Hydraulic Shearing Machine

The Guillotine-type hydraulic shearing machine offers twist-free, precise cutting, particularly for thin sheets at lower rake angles, exceeding rated capacity at higher angles. Its stable, robust design features a ram and table with inclined members aligned to the resultant force, enhancing rigidity.

Safety is ensured with hydraulic and electrical interlocks, while NC upgradation is easily integrated. The bolted square box assembly securely mounts the cylinder, with a guided seal preventing leakage. Sourced from reputable brands, its components ensure reliability, and the ram is guided by six hardened, ground rollers.

Powder Coating

Powder coating is a highly efficient dry finishing process where finely ground polymer powder is electrostatically applied to a surface. Upon heating, the powder melts and fuses into a flawless, durable, and uniform coating. Renowned for its exceptional corrosion resistance and vibrant finish, it outperforms traditional liquid paints in both durability and environmental impact.

Powder coating is eco-friendly, producing minimal VOCs, and allows for the recycling of overspray, reducing waste. Its widespread use spans industries due to its superior performance, sustainability, and aesthetic appeal.
