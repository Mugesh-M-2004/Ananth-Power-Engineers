LTCT METER BOX

Ananth Power Engineers design, manufacture, and supply premium-quality LTCT Meter Boxes, primarily consisting of a meter chamber and a cable chamber. Our LTCT Meter Boxes are meticulously engineered to provide exceptional durability and reliability. Crafted with precision using high-quality materials, these boxes ensure accurate and secure housing for metering equipment.

Designed to withstand harsh environmental conditions, our LTCT Meter Boxes offer superior protection and longevity. Ananth Power Engineers' commitment to excellence ensures that each LTCT Meter Box not only meets but exceeds industry standards, providing robust and efficient solutions for energy metering.

Key Features

  • Dual chamber design (meter and cable chambers)
  • Weather-resistant construction
  • High-quality materials for durability
  • Secure locking mechanism
  • Compliant with industry standards
  • Customizable to specific requirements
